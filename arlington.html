Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use a car buying service in Arlington instead of visiting dealerships myself?

Arlington sits right between Dallas and Fort Worth, which means you're surrounded by hundreds of dealerships all competing for your money. Instead of spending your weekend driving I-20 and Highway 360 from lot to lot, facing high-pressure sales tactics and hidden markups at every stop, we handle the entire process. We work as your personal advocate, negotiating the best possible price so you save time and thousands of dollars.

Do I have to pick up the car at the dealership, or can it be delivered?

Nobody wants to fight I-30 and Highway 360 traffic just to sign paperwork. Depending on the dealership, we can often arrange for your new car to be delivered directly to your home or workplace, whether you're near the Entertainment District, in Mansfield, Grand Prairie, or off Cooper Street. If delivery isn't an option, we ensure all paperwork is 100% ready so your dealership visit takes minutes, not hours.

How do you handle my trade-in, and will I still get the Texas sales tax credit?

Yes! In Texas, trading in your vehicle reduces the taxable amount of your new purchase (you only pay sales tax on the difference). We evaluate your trade-in to ensure you receive top market value, often cross-shopping it against multiple buyers across the entire DFW Metroplex. We factor that value directly into your deal for maximum tax savings without any negotiation headache.

Can you help me find a specific vehicle, like a family SUV or a sporty commuter car?

Absolutely. Whether you need a reliable family SUV for the school run, a fuel-efficient commuter for your drive to Dallas or Fort Worth, or a truck for weekend trips, we know exactly where to look. We track inventory across the entire DFW region to find the exact trim, color, and features you want, even when local lots are running low on popular models.

Do you help negotiate financing rates and extended warranties?

Yes. The finance office is where dealerships often inflate their margins. We review all your financing options, help you compare dealer rates against local credit unions (like EECU, Resource One CU, or Texas Trust CU), and provide honest advice on which protection packages are truly worth your investment.
