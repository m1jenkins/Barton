Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use a car buying service in El Paso instead of just visiting local dealerships?

El Paso's unique geography means dealership options can be spread out across the Borderland. Instead of spending your weekend fighting I-10 traffic or driving over Transmountain (Loop 375) to get from a Westside dealer to one in Horizon City—only to face high-pressure sales tactics and limited local stock—we handle everything. We act as your advocate to negotiate the best price, often sourcing vehicles from a wider region to find exactly what you want at a better price.

Do I have to pick up the car at the dealership, or can it be delivered?

We know your time is valuable. Depending on the dealership, we can often arrange for your new vehicle to be delivered directly to your home or office—whether you're in Socorro, Canutillo, or Horizon City. If delivery isn't an option, we ensure all paperwork is 100% ready beforehand so your dealership visit is a quick 'sign and drive' experience.

How do you handle my trade-in, and will I still get the Texas sales tax credit?

Yes! In Texas, trading in your vehicle reduces the taxable amount of your new purchase (you only pay sales tax on the difference). We evaluate your trade-in to ensure you get top market value—often cross-shopping it against multiple local El Paso buyers. We factor that value directly into your deal for maximum tax savings without the negotiation headache.

Can you help me find a specific vehicle, like a rugged SUV for desert terrain or a fuel-efficient commuter?

Absolutely. Whether you need a rugged Jeep or truck for West Texas terrain or a fuel-efficient hybrid for those long desert drives, we know exactly where to look. We track inventory across the entire region to find the exact trim, color, and features you want, even when local dealership lots are low on stock.

Do you help negotiate financing rates and extended warranties?

Yes. The finance office is where dealerships often increase their margins. We review all your financing options, help you compare dealer rates against local El Paso credit unions (like GECU or First Light Federal Credit Union), and provide honest advice on which protection packages are truly worth your investment.
