Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use a car buying service in Houston instead of just visiting local dealerships?

Houston's sheer size and infamous traffic make car shopping a marathon. Instead of spending your weekend battling I-45 or Beltway 8 traffic to drive from a dealership in The Woodlands to another in Sugar Land—only to face high-pressure sales tactics and hidden add-ons—we handle the entire process. We act as your advocate to negotiate the best possible price, saving you from dealership games and hours of frustration.

Do I have to pick up the car at the dealership, or can it be delivered?

We know navigating the 610 Loop to pick up a car isn't ideal. Depending on the dealership, we can often arrange for your new vehicle to be delivered directly to your driveway or office—whether you're in Katy, Cypress, or Pearland. If dealer delivery isn't available, we ensure all paperwork is 100% ready so your dealership visit takes minutes, not hours.

How do you handle my trade-in, and will I still get the Texas sales tax credit?

Yes! In Texas, trading in your current vehicle reduces the taxable amount of your new car purchase (you only pay sales tax on the difference). We evaluate your trade-in to ensure you get top dollar—often cross-shopping it against multiple local Houston buyers. We factor that value directly into your new deal so you get the maximum tax savings without having to negotiate the trade-in yourself.

Can you help me find a specific vehicle, like a heavy-duty truck or a hybrid for long commutes?

Absolutely. Whether you need a heavy-duty diesel truck for work in the energy sector or a fuel-efficient hybrid for those long Houston commutes, we know exactly where to look. We track inventory across the entire Gulf Coast region to find the exact trim, color, and features you want, even when local lots look empty.

Do you help negotiate financing rates and extended warranties?

Yes. The finance office is where dealerships make significant profit through inflated interest rates. We review all your financing options, help you compare dealer rates against local Houston credit unions (like TDECU, First Community CU, or Houston Federal CU), and advise you honestly on which protection packages are actually worth your money.
