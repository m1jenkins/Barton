Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use a car buying service in Fort Worth instead of visiting dealerships myself?

Fort Worth has dozens of dealerships spread across I-30, I-35W, and the Camp Bowie corridor. Instead of spending your Saturday battling traffic from one lot to the next—dealing with high-pressure tactics and surprise fees at each stop—we handle the entire process. We act as your advocate, negotiating the best possible price on your next vehicle so you save time and thousands of dollars.

Do I have to pick up the car at the dealership, or can it be delivered?

We know nobody wants to battle Chisholm Trail Parkway or I-35W traffic just to sign paperwork. Depending on the dealership, we can often arrange for your new car to be delivered directly to your home or office—whether you're in Southlake, Keller, Benbrook, or the Cultural District. If delivery isn't available, we ensure all paperwork is 100% ready so your visit takes minutes, not hours.

How do you handle my trade-in, and will I still get the Texas sales tax credit?

Yes! In Texas, trading in your vehicle reduces the taxable amount of your new purchase (you only pay sales tax on the difference). We evaluate your trade-in to ensure you receive top market value—often cross-shopping it against multiple buyers across the DFW Metroplex. We factor that value directly into your deal for maximum tax savings without the negotiation headache.

Can you help me find a specific vehicle, like a truck for ranch work or a family SUV?

Absolutely. Whether you need a heavy-duty pickup for ranch roads west of town, a reliable family SUV for the school run to Aledo or Weatherford, or a fuel-efficient commuter for the drive into downtown, we know exactly where to look. We track inventory across the entire North Texas region to find the exact trim, color, and features you want, even when local lots are running low.

Do you help negotiate financing rates and extended warranties?

Yes. The finance office is where dealerships often pad their margins. We review all your financing options, help you compare dealer rates against local Fort Worth credit unions (like Education First FCU, First Command, or Tarrant County CU), and provide honest advice on which protection packages are truly worth your investment.
