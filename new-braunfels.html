Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use a car buying service in New Braunfels instead of just visiting local dealerships?

New Braunfels is one of the fastest-growing cities in the country, and local dealership inventory can't always keep up with demand. Instead of spending your weekend fighting I-35 traffic or navigating the construction on Hwy 46 to visit multiple lots—only to face high-pressure sales tactics and 'market adjustment' markups—we handle everything. We act as your advocate to negotiate the best price, often sourcing cars from across Central Texas to ensure you get exactly what you want without the stress.

Do I have to pick up the car at the dealership, or can it be delivered?

We know that driving into 'town' from the outskirts isn't always convenient. Depending on the dealership, we can often arrange for your new vehicle to be delivered directly to your driveway—whether you're out by Canyon Lake, over in Seguin, or living in Gruene. If dealer delivery isn't available, we ensure all paperwork is 100% ready beforehand so your dealership visit is as fast as a quick errand.

How do you handle my trade-in, and will I still get the Texas sales tax credit?

Yes! In Texas, trading in your current vehicle reduces the taxable amount of your new purchase (you only pay sales tax on the difference). We evaluate your trade-in to ensure you get top market value—often cross-shopping it against multiple local buyers in the Comal County area. We factor that value directly into your deal for maximum tax savings without you having to negotiate the trade-in value yourself.

Can you help me find a specific vehicle, like an SUV for river trips or a truck for the ranch?

Absolutely. Whether you need a spacious SUV for trips to the Comal River or a rugged truck for property out in the Hill Country, we know exactly where to look. We track inventory across the entire region to find the exact trim, color, and features you want, even when local New Braunfels lots look empty.

Do you help negotiate financing rates and extended warranties?

Yes. The finance office is where dealerships often increase their margins. We review all your financing options, help you compare dealer rates against local institutions (like RBFCU or Broadway Bank), and provide honest advice on which protection packages are actually worth your investment.
